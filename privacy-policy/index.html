Privacy Policy

Sondgeroth Fit ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

App Users

  • Personal Information: Email, name (if provided), authentication data.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, workout logs (not personally identifiable).
  • Device Information: iOS version, app version.

Instagram and Social Media (internal business tools only)

  • We use the Instagram Graph API to read public comments on our own Instagram business account posts.
  • This includes publicly visible usernames and comment text from our own posts.
  • We do not collect data from private accounts or access any information beyond what is publicly visible on our posts.

2. How We Use Your Data

App Users

  • To provide and improve our app.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • For analytics and performance tracking.

Instagram and Social Media (internal business tools only)

  • To identify people who have publicly engaged with our content and may be interested in our services.
  • This data is used solely for internal business development purposes and is never published, sold, or shared.
  • We do not automate outreach — all contact decisions are made manually by a human.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use third-party services like:

  • Firebase Authentication for login.
  • Firebase for data storage.
  • RevenueCat for transaction processing.
  • Meta (Instagram Graph API) — reading public comments on our own posts (internal business tool).
  • Anthropic — AI processing for internal content generation (no personal user data is sent).

4. User Rights

You can:

  • Request data deletion or delete it yourself.
  • Update your personal info.
  • Opt out of certain data collection by using local accounts.

5. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: ben@sondgerothfit.com
